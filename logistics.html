Logistics

Lab · Ames Hall

3400 North Charles Street
Ames Hall 108
Baltimore, MD 21218

The Neuroplasticity & Development Lab is in Ames Hall on the Johns Hopkins Homewood campus. Enter campus from North Charles Street; Ames Hall sits near the south end of the academic quad.

Room 108 is on the first floor. If you are arriving for a study visit or meeting, check in with the lab coordinator when you arrive — or email ahead if you need someone to meet you at the building entrance.

  • Building: Ames Hall
  • Room: 108
  • Campus: Homewood
  • Phone: (410) 870-9895
  • Video phone: (410) 214-5087

Scanner · Kennedy Krieger

707 North Broadway
Baltimore, MD 21205

MRI sessions take place at the Kennedy Krieger Institute (KKI) imaging facility in East Baltimore, separate from the Homewood campus lab. Allow extra travel time between Ames Hall and Broadway — these sites are not within walking distance of each other.

Study staff will give you the exact check-in point and arrival time before your scan. If you are driving, follow signage for visitor parking at KKI; if you are taking transit or a rideshare, use 707 N. Broadway as the destination.

  • Facility: Kennedy Krieger Institute
  • Address: 707 N. Broadway
  • Neighborhood: East Baltimore
  • Questions: plasticity_lab@jhu.edu

Before you visit

Participants and guests should confirm which site they need — behavioral testing and most lab meetings are at Ames Hall; imaging visits are at Kennedy Krieger. Contact us if you need help planning travel, accessibility accommodations, or a meet-up point.
