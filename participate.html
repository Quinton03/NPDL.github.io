Participate

Our lab studies how sensory experiences, such as blindness or d/Deafness, and language influence the mind and brain. Our studies involve behavioral testing, functional MRI, and Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation.

We are always recruiting participants and are grateful to anyone willing to take part in the research. If you are interested in participating in our studies, contact us via email at plasticity_lab@jhu.edu, via phone at (410) 870-9895, or via video phone at (410) 214-5087. Potential participants have the option to set up a 30-minute phone or virtual screening, for which they are compensated with a Amazon gift card.
