How does developmental experience affect the human mind and brain?

What is the role of experience in structuring concepts of events, objects and mental states? How are such abstract concepts implemented in neural circuits? Our lab investigates these longstanding philosophical puzzles using the methods of cognitive neuroscience and psychology. We study the origins and structure of human cognition by comparing the minds and brains of people with different developmental experiences.
